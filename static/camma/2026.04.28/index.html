Two other directions

2. Factorized posterior inference

  • CBD localization = posterior inference: $$p(c | x, m_G, m_L) \propto p(x | c, m_G, m_L) p(c | m_G, m_L)$$
  • Context prior: $p(c | m_G, m_L)$

    Where is the CBD likely to be given the two masks?

  • Image based likelihood: $p(x | c, m_G, m_L)$

    Does the image support the presence of the CBD at location $c$ given the two masks?

    where:
  • $c$ : CBD location
  • $x$ : image
  • $m_G$ : gallbladder map
  • $m_L$ : liver map
